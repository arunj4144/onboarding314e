314e Onboarding

Step 1: Sign in to Chrome

Open Chrome and ensure you are signed in with your.email@314ecorp.com email address. This is essential for accessing company resources.

Click the button above to open Gmail. If you're not already signed in, click on the profile icon in the top-right corner of Chrome and sign in with your 314e credentials.

Step 2: Set up 1Password

You will receive an email invitation to set up your 1Password account. Click the link in the email to confirm your account and choose a strong password.

After setting up your password, download and save your Emergency Kit PDF. This contains your Secret Key, which you'll need if you ever forget your password or need to set up 1Password on a new device.

Step 3: Accept Slack Invitation

Check your 314e Gmail for a Slack invitation. Click the link in the email to join the 314e Corp workspace.

Slack is our primary communication tool, so make sure to accept the invitation and familiarize yourself with the platform.

Congratulations!

Your initial onboarding is complete. We're thrilled to have you on board!

Thank you for completing these essential steps. Now, let's move on to the final step: Vanta training.

This is the last step! Please complete the Vanta training, which covers essential security policies and HIPAA awareness.

  • Read and accept our security policies.
  • Complete security awareness & HIPAA training.

The online training should take about 20 minutes. Important: Do not click out of the video or you may have to start over.
